YOUTH & COMPUTER

The Youth & Computer Committee of YWCA of Madras is a dynamic and vibrant initiative aimed at fostering youth involvement and empowerment within our organization and the wider community. Committed to harnessing the potential of young minds and the transformative power of technology, our committee plays a pivotal role in driving positive change and innovation. We conduct lot of Awareness programs and Dental Camps for the beneficiaries

Our Mission:
Youth Engagement:At the Youth & Computer Committee, our mission is to inspire, engage, and empower youth through technology-driven initiatives and community-focused programs. We strive to provide opportunities for skill development, leadership training, and social impact, thereby nurturing the leaders of tomorrow.

Skill Development:We actively involve young individuals in various aspects of our organization, offering them opportunities to volunteer, lead, and contribute to meaningful projects and activities.
Through our computer literacy programs, workshops, and training sessions, we equip youth with essential digital skills needed to thrive in today's digital age. From basic computer skills to advanced technology training, we ensure that young learners are prepared for the challenges and opportunities of the future.

Community Outreach: Beyond the walls of YWCA of Madras, our committee engages in outreach initiatives aimed at making a positive impact in the community. Whether it's organizing computer literacy camps, conducting awareness campaigns on digital literacy, or collaborating with other organizations for social causes, we strive to leverage technology for the greater good.

Get Involved: Are you passionate about youth empowerment and technology? Join us in our mission to make a difference! Whether you're a seasoned professional or a budding enthusiast, there are plenty of opportunities to get involved:

  • Volunteer for our programs and events
  • Share your expertise as a Mentor or Trainer
  • Participate in our Youth led activities
  • Spread the word and advocate for digital inclusion and empowerment
